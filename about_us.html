About Us


As a close-knit group of four friends, we are all passionate about our city. Chicago offers so much and we are really proud of our lively, varied, and deeply historic city. Our common goal was to present Chicago as it truly is--not only as a tourist attraction but also as a location where residents and tourists can mingle and take in the spirit of the city.

We established Windy City Wonders as we want others to view Chicago as we do! We have a strong love for selecting activities that venture beyond the usual tourist route. We cover everything, from the well-known sites to the hidden treasures that only locals are aware of. We hope that by showcasing Chicagoans' talents and stories, we can add significance to your stay and make it an unforgettable experience! Join us as we take you on a tour of the neighborhoods, landmarks, cuisine, and history that make up Chicago.

Windy City Wonders promises to provide inspiration, knowledge, and a genuine experience of the Windy City--whether you're planning your first vacation there or have lived in the city for years.
